Request Account & Data Deletion

You can request the permanent deletion of your ThinkSalon POS account and associated data at any time. Follow the steps below and the ThinkGate team will confirm once the process is complete.

How to submit a deletion request

  1. Send an email to sehaj.kaur.inc@gmail.com with the subject line “ThinkSalon POS Account Deletion”.
  2. Include the following details:
    • Your registered Google Sign-In email address
    • Salon or business name (if applicable)
    • Any backup/export ID if you downloaded data from the app
  3. We will acknowledge your request within 3 business days and delete the account within 7 business days unless a longer retention period is required by law.
  4. Once deletion is complete, we will send you a confirmation email.
What gets deleted?
  • Salon profile details, staff records, and appointment data
  • Products, inventory, invoices, and receipt history stored in our systems
  • Cloud backups and media uploaded through the app

Logs retained for security, fraud prevention, or legal obligations may be kept up to 90 days, after which they are permanently removed.

Need only a partial delete?

If you want specific datasets removed (for example, a single customer profile), mention it in your email and we’ll accommodate the request wherever possible.

Support

If you have questions before submitting a deletion request, write to info@thinkgate.in or call our support line during business hours.
