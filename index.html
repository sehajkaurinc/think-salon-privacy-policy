Privacy Policy · ThinkSalon POS

Last updated: 10 November 2025

ThinkSalon POS (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains, in brief, what information we collect and how we use it when you use our salon management and point-of-sale application.

Information We Collect

  • Account details you provide (name, email, phone, business profile)
  • Salon operations data (services, products, appointments, invoices, payments)
  • Optional media you upload (service images, documents)
  • Device and diagnostic data to improve stability and performance

How We Use Information

  • Deliver POS, billing, appointment, and reporting features
  • Sync data securely across devices using trusted cloud providers
  • Provide customer support and resolve technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our users

Sharing & Storage

We do not sell your information. Data is shared only with essential service providers (e.g., Google Firebase) under strict confidentiality and security measures.

Your Choices

  • Access, update, or delete your data from within the app
  • Export business data using built-in backup/report tools
  • Contact us to request removal or clarification at any time

Contact

For any privacy questions or requests, email us at info@thinkgate.in.
