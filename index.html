ThinkSalon POS Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 November 2025

ThinkSalon POS (“we”, “our”, or “us”) provides salon management and point-of-sale services through our mobile and desktop applications (collectively, the “App”). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Account & Business Information

  • Owner/admin names, email addresses, phone numbers
  • Salon profile details (business name, address, GSTIN, working hours)
  • Staff names, roles, and contact details (optional)

b. Transaction & Appointment Data

  • Services, products, prices, discounts, taxes
  • Appointments, stylist assignments, chair/table numbers
  • Invoices, receipts, payment history

c. Device & Usage Data

  • Device model, OS version, unique device identifiers
  • Log files, crash reports, diagnostic data
  • Approximate location (only if you enable features requiring it)

d. Media & Files (optional)

  • Product or service images you upload
  • CSV or backup files you import/export

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide POS, inventory, appointment, and analytics features
  • Sync data with cloud services for backup and multi-device access
  • Generate invoices, receipts, and business reports
  • Send alerts (appointment reminders, low stock) if enabled
  • Improve the App’s performance, security, and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal information on the basis of contract performance, legitimate interest in providing secure services, and legal compliance. Where required, we obtain your consent for optional features.

4. How We Share Information

  • Service Providers: Google Firebase (authentication, database, storage), Google Analytics, Google Cloud.
  • Payment Partners: If you integrate third-party billing gateways (subject to their policies).
  • Legal Requirements: To comply with lawful requests or protect rights and safety.
  • Business Transfers: In cases of merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with notice provided.

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain business and user data while your account remains active. You can request deletion of data (subject to legal retention requirements). Backup copies may remain for a limited time.

6. Security

  • Transport Layer Security (TLS) for data in transit
  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Regular monitoring for vulnerabilities

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data.

7. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete personal data stored in the App
  • Export business data using built-in reports/backups
  • Opt out of marketing communications (if any) by unsubscribing
  • Revoke app permissions via your device settings

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The “Last updated” date will change accordingly. Continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance.
